What Is ClawPlex

A local AI chapter for people who actually build.

ClawPlex is where Dallas-Fort Worth builders meet to show work, compare notes, and trade practical AI lessons without turning the night into a slide deck parade.

It is for first-time builders, cracked local-model tinkerers, startup teams, and anyone who wants a room with signal instead of hype.

FAQ

First meetup?

Do I need to be an AI expert?

No. Beginners, hobbyists, founders, and senior engineers are all welcome.

What should I bring?

Bring a project, a question, a demo, or just curiosity. A laptop helps but is not required.

Is this only about local models?

No. Local-first AI is part of the culture, but the meetup is broader than one stack choice.
